About the College

Shaping Tomorrow's
Innovators

Marri Laxman Reddy Institute of Technology and Management (MLRITM) is a premier engineering and management institution located in Hyderabad, Telangana.

Affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), the institute offers a broad spectrum of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across engineering, technology, and management disciplines.

MLRITM is committed to fostering innovation, technical excellence, and holistic development — producing engineers and leaders equipped for a rapidly evolving technological world.
